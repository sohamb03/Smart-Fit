Sumo Squats Exercise Guide

01

Wide stance

Stand with your feet wider than shoulder-width apart and toes pointed slightly outward.

02

Hold dumbbell

Grip a single dumbbell with both hands, hanging down in front of you.

03

Squat down

Lower your hips down and back, keeping your chest up and knees in line with your toes.

04

Hold briefly

Pause when your thighs are parallel to the floor and feel the stretch in your inner thighs.

05

Return to start

Drive through your heels to return to standing position. Squeeze your glutes at the top.
