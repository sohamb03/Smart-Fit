Squat Exercise Guide

01

Stand straight

Keep your feet shoulder-width apart and your back straight.

02

Lower down

Push your hips back and bend your knees like you're sitting on a chair.

03

Keep chest up

Make sure your chest is lifted and knees do not go past your toes.

04

Hold position

Pause for a second at the bottom of the squat.

05

Push back up

Drive through your heels to return to standing position.
