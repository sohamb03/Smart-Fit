Lunges Exercise Guide

01

Stand tall

Keep your feet hip-width apart and hands on your hips.

02

Step forward

Take a big step forward with your right leg.

03

Lower your body

Bend both knees to about 90 degrees, front knee over ankle.

04

Push back up

Drive through your front heel to return to start.

05

Repeat other side

Alternate legs and repeat for desired reps.
