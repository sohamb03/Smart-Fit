Deadlift Exercise Guide

01

Set your stance

Stand with feet hip-width apart, bar over midfoot, and grip just outside your legs.

02

Lower your hips

Bend at the hips and knees, keep back flat, and chest up.

03

Lift the bar

Drive through your heels and straighten hips and knees simultaneously.

04

Stand tall

Fully extend hips and knees. Avoid leaning back or shrugging at the top.

05

Return with control

Lower the bar by pushing your hips back and maintaining a flat spine.
