Shoulder Raises Exercise Guide

01

Stand upright

Hold a dumbbell in each hand with arms by your sides, palms facing down.

02

Engage your core

Maintain a straight posture with a slight bend in your elbows.

03

Lift to the sides

Raise both arms sideways until they reach shoulder height.

04

Pause briefly

Hold the raised position for a moment to engage the shoulder muscles.

05

Lower slowly

Return arms to the starting position in a controlled motion.
