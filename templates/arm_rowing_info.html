One Arm Rowing Exercise Guide

01

Position yourself

Place one knee and hand on a bench, keeping your back flat and core tight.

02

Grab the dumbbell

Hold the dumbbell with your free hand, allowing your arm to hang straight down.

03

Row upward

Pull the dumbbell towards your torso, keeping your elbow close to your side. Squeeze your back at the top.

04

Lower slowly

Control the descent, bringing the dumbbell back to the starting position without swinging.
