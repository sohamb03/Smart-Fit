Hammer Curls Exercise Guide

01

Grip neutral

Hold dumbbells with palms facing your torso.

02

Stand tall

Keep your elbows close to your body, spine neutral.

03

Curl upward

Lift dumbbells up by bending your elbows.

04

Squeeze at the top

Pause briefly at the peak of the motion.

05

Lower slowly

Return to start in a controlled manner.
